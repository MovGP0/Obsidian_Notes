$$(i \hbar \gamma^\mu \partial_\mu - m c)\psi = 0$$
Where the wave function $\psi$ is a bi-spinor field:

$$\psi = \begin{bmatrix}
\psi_{1} \\
\psi_{2} \\
\psi_{3} \\
\psi_{4}
\end{bmatrix}$$
Where the **electron spinor** is given by
$$\psi_{e} = \begin{bmatrix}
\psi_{1} \\
\psi_{2}
\end{bmatrix} \in ℂ^2$$
and the **positron spin** is given by
$$\psi_{p} = \begin{bmatrix}
\psi_{3} \\
\psi_{4}
\end{bmatrix} \in ℂ^2$$
