## Properties of i, j, k

$$i \times j = k$$
$$i \times k = -j$$
$$i \times i = 0$$

$$j \times j = 0$$
$$j \times k = i$$
$$j \times i = -k$$

$$k \times j = -i$$
$$k \times k = 0$$
$$k \times i = j$$

$$i^2 = -1$$
$$j^2 = -1$$
$$k^2 = -1$$

## Pauli Matrices

Pauli matrices are 2x2 Hermitian matrices used in quantum mechanics to represent spin operators.

They can be used to replace i, j, k in Quaternions.
$$\sigma_0 = \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 \\
0 & 1
\end{pmatrix}$$
$$\sigma_1 = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & 1 \\
1 & 0
\end{pmatrix}$$
$$\sigma_2 = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & -i \\
i & 0
\end{pmatrix}$$
$$\sigma_2 = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & -i \\
i & 0
\end{pmatrix}$$
